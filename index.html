How to play?

Guess the Overwatch hero in 6 tries.

Each guess must be an existing Overwatch hero.
The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:
    This letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
    This letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    This letter is not in the word in any spot.

Good luck and have fun!
